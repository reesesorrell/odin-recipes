Grilled Cheese and Soup

Desciption

This intricate delicasy provides the tastes that one can only experience through the cookings of chef Reese. Using only the finest Kroger bought soup and cheddar, these two dishes are served perfect besides each other. Tip: try dipping the sandwich in the soup.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheddar
  • A lot of Butter
  • Your Favorite Cambell Soup

Steps

  1. Melt butter on pan and put cheddar on bread.
  2. Toast one side of sandwich until golden brown.
  3. Remove from pan and add more butter.
  4. Stick soup in microwave for recommended amount of time.
  5. Toast other side of sandwich until golden brown and remove soup from microwave.
